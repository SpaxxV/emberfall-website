About Us

Built by the
Community.

Emberfall is an up-and-coming Minecraft civilization event server, established September 2025 and reaching 1000+ members after only 5 months!

Emberfall hosts multi-day modded civilization events with custom maps and modpacks. These events mostly focus on the roleplay side of Minecraft, but does include elements of PVP.

The Discord server has a loyal community and an active staff team who is heavily engaged in the community. Join up and become a part of it all!
